Tempify

Tempify is an app that allows workers and businesses to easily create employment agreements and schedule work. We're the employer, so we handle the paperwork and benefits. We enable our users to manage their work relationships as they've done in the past while behind the scenes we make sure both businesses and workers are compliant with laws and regulations.

Cordatas

Cordatas is a fully integrated toolset for companies working with computationally-heavy data! With Cordatas, you can cluster computers, maintain and manage the cluster, load and offload experiments, and even perform live and post-analysis on your data all from a central location! In addition, we are also working to make our software compliant with the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) for the Department of Defense (DoD) allowing researchers to easily integrate our software for DoD contracts.
